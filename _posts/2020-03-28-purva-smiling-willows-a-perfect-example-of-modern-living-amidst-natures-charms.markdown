Puravankara Limited is a real estate development group that aims at delivering beautiful and peaceful residential apartments that are inspired by natural surroundings. With 45 years of experience in real estate development, they have come up with a new luxurious project named Purva Smiling Willows which enjoys a natural ambiance.
Purva Smiling Willows offers luxurious villas that are nestled amongst thick green foliage spread over 20 acres that showcase the finest elements of the rainforest. The project is located off Bannerghatta Road in Chikkakammana Halli. The main highlight of the project is man-made creations and nature’s miracles that surround the project with trees, swirling streams, and therapeutic waterfalls. The project configurations are 3 BHK and 4 BHK. Purva Smiling Willows aims at providing apartments to homebuyers who have an appreciation for the natural environment. The location advantage of Purva Smiling Willows lies in the proximity of the facilities nearby such as schools, colleges, hospitals, recreation areas, and parks. The Group aims at delivering superior and comfortable homes to all homebuyers through extraordinary and imaginative spaces created out of deep customer focus and insight.
